    What are the Philhealth Partners?

    PhilHealth collaborates with to fulfill its mandate of providing universal health coverage and promoting health equity in the Philippines. By leveraging partnerships with diverse stakeholders, PhilHealth aims to strengthen its impact and reach in improving the health and well-being of all Filipinos.

    Learn More

    Government Agencies

    PhilHealth collaborates with government agencies at the national and local levels to coordinate health policies, programs, and initiatives. This includes partnerships with the Department of Health (DOH), local government units (LGUs), and other agencies involved in healthcare delivery and public health promotio

    Professional Associations

    PhilHealth partners with professional associations representing healthcare professionals, such as physicians, nurses, and allied health professionals. These associations help disseminate information about PhilHealth programs and policies, provide training and education to their members, and advocate for the interests of healthcare providers and patients.

    Technology Partners

    PhilHealth partners with technology companies and vendors to develop and maintain information systems, databases, and digital platforms for managing health insurance claims, membership records, and other administrative functions. These technology partners help PhilHealth streamline processes, improve efficiency, and enhance service delivery to members and providers.
